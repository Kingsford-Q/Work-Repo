Strategy

A four-stage process, repeated deliberately.

We don't diversify into commercial, student blocks or serviced accommodation. Every acquisition follows the same four stages, on the same handful of postcodes.

01

Sourcing

We track auction listings, probate sales and tired rental stock across BD and LS postcodes, underwriting each on post-refurbishment value before an offer is made.

Off-market
02

Refurbishment

A fixed contractor bench handles works to a consistent specification — no scope creep, no surprise invoices halfway through.

6–10 wks
03

Letting

Properties are let directly, with referencing run in-house rather than outsourced to a local agent chasing commission.

Direct-let
04

Hold & report

Each property is underwritten as a minimum seven-year hold, with annual valuations and rent reviews rather than short-term flips.

7yr+ hold
Approach
"The properties that scare most buyers off — damp, an outdated kitchen, a probate sale with no chain — are usually the ones with the widest margin once the work is priced properly."
— Ibrahim Azam, Director, RK Investment Group

RK Investment Group Ltd was incorporated in April 2024 as a single-purpose vehicle for buy-to-let acquisition across West Yorkshire. It is deliberately small: one director, one class of share, and a short list of postcodes we know street by street.

We turn down more properties than we buy. A deal only proceeds once refurbishment cost, letting demand and exit value have all been checked independently — not estimated once and hoped for.
