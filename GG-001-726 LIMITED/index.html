Services

What we take off an owner's plate.

Four disciplines, run in-house rather than farmed out to whoever answers the phone first.

01 · Sourcing

Acquisition and due diligence

We shortlist stock that meets our own letting criteria before an offer is ever made — not the other way round.

Off-market and early-listing access across W6, W4 and SW6
Structural and lease-length checks before instruction
Realistic rent modelling, not agent optimism
02 · Management

Day-to-day portfolio management

Maintenance, safety compliance, contractor coordination and rent collection, handled without the owner needing to field a single call.

Gas, electrical and EPC compliance tracked to renewal date
Vetted contractor bench for repairs under 48 hours
Monthly statements, not quarterly surprises
03 · Lettings

Tenant sourcing and relations

Reference checks run properly, tenancies pitched at a rent that actually renews, and a single point of contact for the length of the let.

Right-to-rent and credit referencing on every applicant
Renewal conversations start 90 days ahead of expiry
Direct line for tenants — no call centre queue
04 · Reporting

Compliance and owner reporting

Records held to a standard that survives an audit, an insurer's query, or a call from HMRC — not just a landlord's memory.

Digital records for every certificate and invoice
Annual portfolio review with yield and cost breakdown
Direct liaison with company secretary on statutory filings
Approach

Run like a hospitality floor, not a filing cabinet.

Our principal built her career in food service, where a late delivery or a cold plate is never someone else's problem. We apply the same standard here: a leak reported at 9pm gets a contractor by morning, not a ticket number.
