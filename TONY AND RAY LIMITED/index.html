About

Two directors, equal stake, no silent partner.

Tony and Ray Limited was incorporated in April 2024 by Rahmatullah Hossiany and Nertil Troka, who each hold half the company and both sit on every significant decision — from which properties get instructed to how a difficult tenancy gets handled.

The name is a nod to the old-fashioned, two-founder agencies that used to exist on every high street before consolidation swallowed most of them. The service model is the same idea, updated: one point of contact, from valuation through to the tenancy renewal three years later.

Director · Sales & Acquisitions

Leads valuations and sales negotiations, and holds the primary landlord relationships across the portfolio.

Nertil Troka

Runs day-to-day lettings, compliance and the maintenance contractor network.
